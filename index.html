About Heather

Two Decades Shaping
Real Asset Investment

Heather Fernstrom is an award-winning entrepreneur and nationally recognized real estate investment advisor with over twenty years in the private and public real estate sector. She co-founded Alliance Global Advisors in 2020, building it into one of the fastest-growing strategic advisory platforms in the industry.

Prior to Alliance, Heather served as Managing Director for National Real Estate Advisors and held leadership positions at Hunt Investment Management and The Townsend Group. She speaks at leading industry conferences and is a passionate board member of Wellfit Girls and the American Heart Association.
